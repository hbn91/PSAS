Privacy Policy and Data Usage

All data collected for this is to be used solely by Ashley Harbin ("The Developer") for the fulfillment of the Project module of the BSc (Hons) Computer Science programme at the University of Lincoln.

All data is stored within GDPR and the ethical guidelines provided by the University and will consist only of the Student ID number for email login ID purposes. This will be held psuedoanonymised for this purpose only. Responses and data may be used for the purposes of evaluation, testing and the resulting dissertation but all responses will be anonymised. No other personally identifying information such as name, age shall be collected.

Users retain the right to withdraw their consent of participation in this project and that their data be deleted upon request. Requests for this should be sent to The Developer at 17652530@students.lincoln.ac.uk WITH the SUBJECT LINE stating PSAS Withdrawal, and the user ID and statement.

Data will be held for no longer than is necessary for the duration of this project evaluation and testing and shall be deleted upon completion of the degree program.
